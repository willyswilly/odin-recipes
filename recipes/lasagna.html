Spinach Lasagna

Description

Here is a famous lasagna recipe by the Tran dynasty

Ingredients

  • 6 pieces of Lasagna Noodles
  • Spinach
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Boil the Lasagna Noodles
  2. Combine Spinach, Eggs, Ricotta, Mozarella, Parmesan and Salt and Pepper
  3. Lay one scoop of tomato sauce in the baking pan
